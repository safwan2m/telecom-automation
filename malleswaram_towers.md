# Malleswaram Tower Survey

**Area:** Malleswaram, North Bangalore  
**Bounding box:** 12.983–13.022 N, 77.538–77.600 E  
**Source:** TRAI Tarang Sanchar screenshot (2026-06-14) — blue pins = physical tower sites  
**Total towers identified:** ~90 (all operators combined)  

---

## Instructions for field verification

1. Open [Tarang Sanchar EMF Portal](https://tarangsanchar.gov.in/EMFPortal/SiteFinder/Verify), complete email verification
2. Navigate to each approximate lat/lon below and click the nearest blue pin
3. Fill in **Operator**, **Technology**, and **Tower ID** from the popup
4. Correct the lat/lon from the popup if it differs from the estimate
5. Add any towers visible in the portal that are missing from this list

**Operator codes to use:** `Airtel` · `Jio` · `Vi` · `BSNL` · `Unknown`  
**Technology codes:** `LTE` · `UMTS` · `GSM` · `LTE+UMTS` · `All`
